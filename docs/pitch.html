SoulCap

A quiet, private emotional operating system: detailed check-ins, inspectable local patterns, 37 guided skills, six searchable offline articles, optional no-streak daily supports, a journal book, and a map of the people around you. No account. No server. No analytics.

Honesty. SoulCap is self-guided wellness software — not therapy, diagnosis, treatment, or a crisis service. Everything in the shipped PWA stays in localStorage. Nest and Expo remain separate, undeployed labs.
